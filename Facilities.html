Facilities in AMAYA-LAKE

Accommodation & Hospitality
 ✔ Luxury Villas & Chalets – Spacious rooms with traditional Sri Lankan décor and modern comforts.
✔ Private Plunge Pools (in select villas) – Exclusive and serene spaces for relaxation.
✔ 24/7 Room Service – Personalized services to ensure a comfortable stay.
✔ Concierge & Tour Desk – Assistance with bookings, excursions, and travel planning.


Dining & Culinary Experiences
 ✔ Samara Restaurant – A fine dining experience featuring Sri Lankan and international cuisine.
✔ Lounge Bar – A relaxing spot to enjoy cocktails and beverages with scenic views.
✔ Lakeside Dining – Private dining experiences by the tranquil Kandalama Lake.
✔ Sri Lankan Cooking Classes – Learn to prepare authentic Sri Lankan dishes with local chefs.


Spa & Wellness
 ✔ Ayurveda Spa – Traditional Sri Lankan therapies and massages for relaxation.
✔ Yoga & Meditation – Sessions to rejuvenate the mind and body in a natural setting.
✔ Swimming Pool – A large outdoor pool surrounded by tropical greenery.
✔ Fitness Center – Well-equipped gym for workout sessions.

Adventure & Outdoor Activities
 ✔ Cycling & Nature Walks – Explore scenic trails around the lake and forest.
✔ Boat Rides on Kandalama Lake – A peaceful and scenic experience.
✔ Birdwatching – Discover a variety of exotic birds in the resort’s natural surroundings.
✔ Jeep Safari to Minneriya National Park – Witness Sri Lanka’s famous elephant gathering.
✔ Excursions to Sigiriya & Dambulla Cave Temple – Guided tours to UNESCO World Heritage sites.
