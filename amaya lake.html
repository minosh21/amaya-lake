AMAYA LAKE - DAMBULLA



Embrace a lakefront paradise at one of Sri Lanka’s best resorts with glistening views of the Kandalama lake.













A forest-laden pathway leads to the enchanting seclusion of Amaya Lake, one of Dambulla’s best hotels. Located along the shores of the Kandalama reservoir and within close proximity to a number of local attractions including the UNESCO world heritage site Sigiriya, Amaya Lake is one of the most coveted Dambulla hotels. Just 140 km (86 miles) from Bandaranaike International Airport, this luxury holiday resort offers a scenic 3.5-hour drive through the heart of Sri Lanka.
